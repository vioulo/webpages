Mailchimp Integration

1. Go to www.mailchimp.com and login.
2. Simply go to "My Templates".
3. Click "Import Template" and choose the template in the MailChimp Template folder.
4. Click on "Upload" and you're done!

Campaign Monitor Integration

1. Go to www.campaignmonitor.com/ and login.
2. Simply go to "Templates".
3. Click "Add a new template".
4. Choose to Import your own template by clicking on "Import it now".
5. In the HTML page section choose the template in the Campaign Monitor Template folder.
6. Click on "Add template" and you're done!
